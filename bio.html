James Villas is a 24 years old singer, songwriter, instrumentalist, and aspiring audio-engineer born in Davao Philippines on 13th of October 1996.

Born and raised in the Philippines. James grew up in a place where everyone is talented, gifted, and competitive. 

One of the mentalities in his surrounding is, if you can't hit a high note you can'tsing. That characteristic has grown into him and made him a competitive person.

He always loved the music even when he was a young kid. James has been singing, dancing and has been introduced to some instruments since he was a young kid. James have not decided to jump into music in his early age because of his doubts when it comes to his capabilities.

During his teenage years James used to be in dance crew both in The Philippines and The Netherlands. When he turned 21 years old James slowly realized how much passion he has when it comes to singing and slowly have had more interest with playing instruments. Two years later he has been introduced to a great Music Academy and fantastic vocal coach where he could grow even further in music and decided to live his dream.

He grew up listening to 70’s, 80’s, 90’s and 2000’s music. The genre can go from Filipino songs, to Alternative rock to RnB music . This is because everyone in the Philippines loves singing and loves karaoke's.

As he grew older, he has been heavily influenced by RnB, Soul, Neo-Soul, and Jazz artist. To name a few these are the artist:

  • D’Angelo
  • Miguel
  • Sir
  • Giveon
  • Tom Misch

The list can go on!

What you can expect from James is not just his artistry but also, diversity of musicality. He also aspire to inspire and motivate others to do what they love and show they craft regardless of the cirumstances.

​James’ artistry is based on his experiences, struggles, growth, love, and emotions. James aspire to influence people through his music, word, action, and performance.
